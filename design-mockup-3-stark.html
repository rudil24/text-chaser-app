Design Specification
Proposed Name
Still
From "to be still" — quiet, unmoving.
Also "still water", "still life", "still here."
The name holds two meanings in tension: the calm the app offers, and the condition required to survive it. You must keep moving to stay still in existence. The moment you become still — you vanish.

UI Component Behavior
Timer — Idle Warning (5s → 3s)
Timer bar is neutral warm gray. Digits are hidden. The absence of urgency is its own quiet. Nothing alerts the writer until the threat is real.
Timer — Danger Zone (3s → 0s)
Bar transitions to #C0392B. Digits appear and pulse with a slow 600ms breathe-in / breathe-out. The background of the editor canvas begins a barely-perceptible warm tint cycle — the room itself holds its breath.
Deletion — Fade to White
An overexposed white fills the canvas in 400ms. Text ghosts beneath, blurring and dissolving simultaneously. After 800ms, the aftermath message fades in from silence: "The page is clean."
Cursor
Standard blink (1s step-end). In danger state, blink accelerates to 0.4s, becoming a flicker. Mimics a candle about to go out. Subtle. Devastating.
Typing Recovery
The moment a key is pressed, all danger states reset in 80ms: bar refills with an elastic ease, the red fades to warm gray, the tint lifts. The relief is immediate and visceral — the reward for not stopping.
Start Screen
Completely still. No motion whatsoever. The radial gradient at rest. The single button waits. The calm before is total — and therefore the contrast, once the timer begins, is total.
